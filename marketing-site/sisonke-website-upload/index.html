A clear, transparent claim workflow

Every claim follows a governed, multi-role process — from submission to payout, with a complete audit trail at every stage.

1

Member submits

Upload the death certificate and capture claim details for the deceased.

2

Secretary reviews

Eligibility check, document review and a formal recommendation.

3

Chairperson decides

Final approval or rejection with mandatory decision notes recorded.

4

Treasurer pays

Marks the payout with a reference number, amount and timestamp.

Why Sisonke is different

PEO Capital Holdings didn’t bolt community finance onto a generic app. Sisonke was designed from the ground up around how South African societies, stokvels and community groups actually operate.

✓

Constitution and rule-driven logic

Waiting periods, dependent limits and contribution amounts are configured per society and enforced automatically in every workflow.

✓

Strict role-based access

Members see member things. Secretaries administer. Chairpersons approve. Treasurers pay. No role confusion, no unauthorised access.

✓

Treasurer-only payment marking

Only a verified Treasurer can mark a claim as paid, with a mandatory reference number and payout amount for every transaction.

✓

Agenda-linked minutes

Minutes are built from agenda discussion notes — not typed from scratch. The Secretary edits, the Chairperson approves, members view.

✓

Claim eligibility assessment

Before approval, the system checks waiting periods, member standing, dependent coverage and outstanding contribution arrears.

✓

Governance timeline

Every status change — warning, suspension, expulsion — is tracked with a reason, the acting member and a timestamp.
