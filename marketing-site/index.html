Why Sisonke is different

PEO Capital Holdings didn't bolt community finance onto a generic app. Sisonke was designed from the ground up around how South African societies, stokvels and community groups actually operate.

✓

Constitution and rule-driven logic

Operating rules — waiting periods, dependent limits, contribution amounts — are configured per society and enforced in every workflow automatically.

✓

Strict role-based access

Members see member things. Secretaries administer. Chairpersons approve. Treasurers pay. No role confusion and no unauthorised access.

✓

Treasurer-only payment marking

Only a verified Treasurer can mark a claim payout as paid, with a mandatory reference number and payout amount for every transaction.

✓

Agenda-linked minutes

Minutes are built from agenda discussion notes — not typed from scratch. The Secretary edits, the Chairperson approves, members view once published.

✓

Claim eligibility assessment

Before approval, the system checks waiting period satisfaction, member standing, dependent coverage and outstanding contribution arrears.

✓

Governance timeline

Every status change — warning issued, suspension, expulsion — is tracked with a reason, the acting member and a timestamp. Full accountability.
